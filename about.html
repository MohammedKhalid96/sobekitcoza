    • Our Story

    Sobek IT is a proud member of the Sobek Group of companies. Our group is specialized in infrastructure; with decades of experience, we felt the time was right to delve into the virtual and smart cities and operations and provide a smarter solution and sustainable outlook. We do not wish to create a technological world, but a smarter world where creativity, sustainability and opportunities become a regular and continuous mechanism.

    ​ In our continuously digitalising world, we seek to provide meaningful and relevant smart solutions to everyday problems our societies and communities may face. We seek to make the lives our communities and others better by adding value and innovation.
