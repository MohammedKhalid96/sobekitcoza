ABOUT US

Sobek IT (Pty) Ltd. is a virtual infrastructure company; headquartered in Johannesburg, South Africa. However, our branches extend to Northern America, Europe and Africa.

We are, proudly, part of Sobek Group; specialized in infrastructure. Sobek IT is a virtual infrastructure and digital solution provider company with emphasis on smart cities and smart operations.

Sobek IT seeks to continuously provide solutions that are

  • cost-effective,
  • competent,
  • advanced,
  • accessible and
  • easily-implemented.

Our services are driven by a local market analysis within any international region serving the diverse requirements of a developing and changing world
